Dreams come alive when little faces are first lit up by this world-famous, iconic Disney landmark.
A Fairytale Come True: Sleeping Beauty Castle Sleeping Beauty Castle, rising up in the sky at the end of Main Street, U.S.A®, invites you to a beautiful realm of imagination and pleasure. The Castle, the central symbol of Disneyland Paris, welcomes all visitors to rejoice in a place where dreams will come true. The 167-foot tower, which features towering spires, ornate towers, regal royal-blue rooftops, stained glass windows, and tapestries, evokes both the charm and intrigue of imaginary castles such as the one seen in Disney's animated classic Sleeping Beauty, as well as elements from actual French castles and heritage sites. The castle is surrounded by square trees and is home to a huge sleeping dragon who periodically awakens, puffs smoke, and roars, adding to the magical atmosphere.




















Tour the famed Hollywood Tower Hotel, which was once a hangout for Tinseltown's rich and famous before it was forced to shutter in 1939 due to an unknown incident. At The Twilight Zone Tower of Terror, enter through the iron-wrought iron gates of the deserted abode and into a dusty lobby that seems to be stuck in time. Enter a mysterious library filled with odd objects. As lightning strikes the darkened building, a nearby television set come alive, and Rod Serling invites you to the Twilight Zone. Are you going up? Sitting in the elevator, ascend in the dark before the corroded doors open to reveal a long corridor occupied by the otherworldly hotel residents from 1939. The eerie spectres dematerialize, beckoning you to join them, and the skyward journey ends. Going downhill! Whatever horror terrifies your mind, one aspect is certain: you will scream in panic as the lift drifts down the deserted shaft until abruptly shifting direction and shooting back up the hotel. Look out of a 13-story-high hole in the wall as the doors open and shut mysteriously to expose the hotel grounds. And, just after you recover your breath, the elevator declines down and speeds back up, the sound of wires breaking and metal jingling above. Will you be able to return to reality... or will you become a lifelong resident of The Twilight Zone?
